
Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

---

Do’s -

1. Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.  
2. Keep your profile privacy settings updated.  
3. Think before posting any content online.
4. Share useful, informative, and positive content.
5. Respects other and communicate very politely.

---

Don’ts

1. Do not share personal information.  
2. Do not post offensive or harmful content.  
3. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.  
4. Do not spread fake news and unverified information.  
5. Do not overshare your location or daily activities.  

---

Note

Following these guideline helps to maintain a safe, respectful, and positive online presence.

---

Conclusion

Following these simple do’s and don’ts can help students to maintain a safe digital presence, protect their privacy, and build a positive online identity.
