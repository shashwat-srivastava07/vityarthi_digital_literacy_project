#  Stay Safe Online: Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

> Cybercrime is increasing rapidly. Follow these guidelines to stay safe online.

---

##  General Safety Tips
1. Use strong and unique password. 
2. Enable two-factor authentication.
3. Avoid clicking on suspicious links.  
4. Keep your apps and devices updated. 

---

# Financial / UPI Safety Tips
1. Never approve unknown payment requests  
2. Do not share UPI PIN or OTP  
3. Always verify receiver details 
4. Avoid public Wi-Fi for transactions  

---

# Awareness & Smart Behavior
✔ Be cautious of fake calls claiming to be from banks  
✔ Ignore offers that seem too good   
✔ Always check website URLs before logging in  
✔ Log out from public devices  

---

## Social Media Safety
✔ Do not hare personal information  
✔ Regularly update privacy settings  
✔ Avoid accepting requests from unknown people  


---

## Final Tip
> **Think Before You Click!**  
> Staying alert and informed is the best way to protect yourself from cyber threats.
